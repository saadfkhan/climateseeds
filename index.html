How it Works

Selected founders get a stipend of £2500 per month the first two months.
We invest £120,000 for a 10% equity stake in each company selected by our investment committee.
For all companies we invest in, £40,000 is used to cover the program fees so you get £80,000
in the bank to start building the foundations of a great business.

The Founders

Chris Coleridge

Program Lead UK

Chris is Senior Faculty in Management Practice at Cambridge Judge Business School. A former entrepreneur, for 13 years he has been teaching and carrying out advisory work in entrepreneurship and strategy, inside and outside academia. He has won multiple awards for his teaching, sits on startup advisory boards and advises corporates on partnering/ecosystem strategy. He holds an MBA from London Business School, an MSc in Social Psychology and a PhD in Management from the London School of Economics.

Nicky Dee

Sustainability Director

From funding mechanisms for new ventures, opportunity screening for eco-innovation, structuring low carbon innovation prizes, and direct input to new ventures, Nicky has focused on the support of new ventures. This has included work with Climate KIC, government departments, Carbon Trust, Enecsys, the European Commission, the University of Cambridge and Colleges, NESTA and others. Nicky has a PhD in environmental technology management from the University of Cambridge and teaches on this and related topics. Nicky’s recent research has examined sustainability from an industrial emergence perspective, with an emphasis on the role of finance. ​

Frank Knowles

Finance Expert

Frank is a social impact angel investor. He has over 30 years experience in financial services as an investor, top-ranked high yield credit analyst and adviser. Most recently he was a founding partner in New Street Research, the leading global independent TMT research provider to institutional investors.​ Frank has an MA in Natural Sciences from Cambridge University, and is studying for a Diploma in Entrepreneurship at the Judge Business School.

André Schwaninger

Program Lead Europe

Andre is a former Accenture MD with 20yrs of international consulting experience in Energy, Utilities, Oil & Gas, Pharmaceutical, High Tech, and Automotive-Industries. Andre has a proven track record of managing complex programs and teams with a strong "can do attitude". Andre is a founder of a digital consulting business and proficient in Digital Transformation, Business & Practice Development. He holds a Master in Physics from ETH Zurich and a Diploma in Entrepreneurship (Cambridge Judge).
